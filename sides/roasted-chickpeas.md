# Roasted Chickpeas

## Ingredients

- A can of chickpeas
- Oil
- Spices (e.g. garlic powder, salt...)

## Instructions

1. Rinse the chickpeas
2. Dry the chickpeas: the drier you get them, the better. Remove all skins that come off.
3. Roast the chickpeas on a dry pan until they are slightly crispy. Mix frequently.
4. Add the oil and spices, mix well and fry for a bit more.
5. Enjoy!
