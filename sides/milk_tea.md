## Bubble Milk Tea

### Ingredients
- bubbles
- milk
- sugar
- tea
- optional: toppings

### Preparations
- Boil per package (usually 20–30 min simmer + 15–20 min rest).
- Heat 300 ml (1 1/4 cups) water to a boil.
- Add tea.
- Steep 4–5 minutes for strong flavor. Remove bags/strain leaves.
- Sweeten while hot with 2–4 tbsp syrup (to taste). Cool slightly for iced; keep hot for hot version.


Enjoy!
