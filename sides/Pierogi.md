# Pierogi recipe


## Ingredients

### Dough
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 large egg
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 2 Tbsp sour cream (optional, for softer dough)

### Filling
- 1 lb potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 cup farmer's cheese or sharp cheddar, grated
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 2 Tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

### To Serve
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1 onion, sliced
- Sour cream


## Instructions

- Boil potatoes until tender, then mash
- Fry chopped onion in butter until golden, add half to mashed potatoes
- Mix in cheese, salt and pepper to the potato mixture
- Combine flour and salt, make a well, add egg and water
- Knead dough until smooth, rest 20 minutes
- Roll dough thin, cut circles, add filling
- Fold and seal edges firmly
- Boil pierogi in salted water until they float (3-4 minutes)
- Fry sliced onion in butter until golden, add boiled pierogi
- Lightly fry pierogi with onions until lightly crisped
- enjoy!
