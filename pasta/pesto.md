# Pesto pasta recipe

## Pasta 

- 300g spaghetti

## Sauce

- 2 pots of basil
- 75g pine nuts
- 100g parmesan
- 3-4tbsp olive oil
- lemon juice to taste

## Instructions

- Mix all ingredients for the sauce in a blender or by hand until it has a smooth consistency. Cook pasta with lot of salted water. Mix pasta together with sauce and enjoy!
