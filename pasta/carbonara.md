# Pasta carbonara recipe

## Ingredients

- spaghetti numero 3
- guanciale
- two egg yolks
- pecorino
- olive oil
- black pepper

## Istructions

- dice the guangiale and cook it in a frying pan with a drop of olive oil
- separate the yolks from the whites and mix them
- add the pecorino to the yolks and mix vigorously
- add black pepper to the mix
- boil the water, for every 100g of pasta have 1 liter of water
- lots of salt in the water, the water has to taste like sea water
- read the boiling time on the pasta box. Use only italian pasta. Recommendations: Rummo, Molisana, Voiello, Garofalo.
