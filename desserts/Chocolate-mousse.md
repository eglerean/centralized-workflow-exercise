# Chocolate-mousse  recipe

## Ingredients

- Dark chocolate (150g)
- Whipped cream (1 cup)
- Salt (pinch)
- Espresso (Optional)

## Preparation
- Melt dark chocolate
- Fold whipped cream in a cup
- Add a pinch of salt
- Chill 30 min

## Instructions

- Melt 150 g dark chocolate
- Fold into 1 cup whipped cream with a pinch of salt
- Chill 30 min
- Add 1 tbsp espresso (Optional)
